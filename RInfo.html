The Recipe App

The idea for this app came from my family never knowing what to eat for dinner we would have stuff in the fridge but have no ideas. The app has to pages one for food and one for drink . You type in an ingrediant, for food that could be beef or chicken and for drinks that could be a type of alchole it then uses an API to access a food and drink database and gives you a list of things you can make with your desired ingrediant
